Another advantage is that you simply have to speak the language if you are with other people. In your home country, it will always be possible to speak Czech in class if you want to, so the learning may be slower.
On the other hand, there are also advantages to staying at home to study. You don’t have to make big changes to your life, and it is also a lot cheaper than going to Britain. If you have a really good English teacher at school, you can learn in a more concentrated way compared to, for example, taking a short-term course in Britain. And, of course, today’s digital technologies allow studying English from the comfort of your home, so why not take up an e-learning course?
